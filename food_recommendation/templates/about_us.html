We are FlickO!

Providing you with the best food recommendations!

At FlickO, we want to simplify the food ordering process for you.

We understand how time-consuming and tedious it can be to choose what to eat and where to order from. That's why we provide you with the best food recommendations based on your current mood, as well as suggest the best restaurant for the tastiest experience using our advanced ML models.

We believe that time is money, so why waste 15 minutes or more each time you order food online? Our innovative technology can help you find the perfect meal in less than a minute, with recommendations that are backed by data. We analyze the data you provide us and compare it with a vast universe dataset of other users. Our models then generate the best food recommendations and the best restaurants, based on multiple ratings and reviews.

Why go through the hassle of figuring out what to eat and where to order from when FlickO can do all the work for you? Our platform takes the guesswork out of food ordering, so you can sit back and enjoy your meal.

But wait, there's more! We are always looking for ways to improve our platform and make your food ordering experience even better. In the near future, we will be introducing a cost comparison feature, which will suggest the food ordering platform that offers the lowest price for your selected meal. This way, you can save a few bucks without compromising on quality.
